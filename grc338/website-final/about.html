About Talk Taiyaki

Welcome to Talk Taiyaki, where passion meets craftsmanship to create a dessert experience like no other. Specializing in Asian-inspired treats, we pride ourselves on using the finest ingredients and traditional techniques, infused with a modern touch. Our menu features everything from golden taiyaki cones filled with creamy soft serve to fluffy mochi donuts and artisanal bubble teas. Each creation is made fresh daily, ensuring every bite is bursting with flavor and texture that will leave you coming back for more.

Talk Taiyaki was founded by a husband-and-wife duo who share a deep love for food and community. With roots in both Asian and Western culinary traditions, they’ve combined their expertise and passion to create a space that celebrates the best of both worlds. Their vision is to provide not just desserts but also a place where people can connect, share stories, and create lasting memories. Every detail, from the menu to the warm ambiance, reflects their commitment to quality and their belief that food has the power to bring people together.

At Talk Taiyaki, your experience is our priority. From the moment you step in, our friendly team is dedicated to making your visit special. Whether you’re indulging in a quick treat or settling in with friends and family, we aim to provide exceptional service and a welcoming atmosphere that feels like home. So, join us for a delicious journey filled with sweet surprises and heartfelt connections—because at Talk Taiyaki, every visit is a celebration of flavor, quality, and community.
